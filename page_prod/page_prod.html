Supernova (2023)

par Sacha Vučinić


After an accident disrupts Axel's life, a passionate dancer turned quadriplegic, he confronts his family and friends with the heart-wrenching choice of end-of-life. As relationships are tested, his twin sister Lumia finds herself at the center of a moral dilemma, torn between respecting her brother's will and facing the complex emotions that accompany this decision. This exploration delves into the boundaries of love, sacrifice, and resilience.
