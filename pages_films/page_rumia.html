Rumia

a movie by Anthony Vazquez

Sofi, a young, ambitious, immigrant dance teacher has always been clear about one thing: she will not have children. Even after finally meeting a man who loves her the way she’s always dreamed of, her stance remains firm. When the relationship suddenly comes to an end and she begins to show early signs of pregnancy, her world is turned upside down. Grappling with her new reality, Sofi questions and reevaluates her core values as she attempts to make the right decision.
