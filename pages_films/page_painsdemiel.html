Pains de Miel

a movie by Océane Combeau

Three couples spend the afternoon together at a table. Under smiling faces and nice words, each of them fight against their own demons and hide them as well as possible. Whether words free or blame, each will use this table as a public place to examine or take a witness. Group, couple or individual, three ways of being and appearing that exist in parallel but where the truth resides between the lines. What we show, what we hide.
