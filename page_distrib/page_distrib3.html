Les Eveillées (2022)

by Nina Bouchaud-Cheval


Zoe, a lonely teenager, escapes a painful family life by spending time with a community of travelers. When her father suddenly dies, France, a rebellious young Romany, takes her on the road to organise a traditional wake.

Sad Diva Lands On The Moon (2023)

by Moe Myat May Zarchi


Sad Diva Lands on the Moon follows the escapism of May, a young girl caught up in the turmoil of her country. While she stays at home, doing laundry or surfing the internet, her emotions take an imaginative run in absurd scenarios to forget the reality she leaves behind. Her journey takes her to the moon, while she tries to save a brainwashed, sad diva stuck in an old propaganda TV show.

Alpa (2022)

by Paolo Mattei


In a remote village of Corsica, Antoine, a young shepherd in his twenties, walks through life without enthusiasm. One day, while hunting, he meets Achille, a young runaway who hides in the scrubland. A new life is possible: Antoine can either flee the village or stay. Achille will not give him the option.
